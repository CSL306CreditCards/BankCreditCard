Autopay Options

Make things a lot easier with automatic payments. Set Autopay once and pay your bills everytime on time.

  • Convenience
    No need to track payment due dates. Set 'AutoPay' today to forget all worries.
  • Additional Security
    Set AutoPay for a specific payment limit or for the total amount due & manage your payments better.
  • SMS & Email Alerts
    Get regular SMS & Email alerts on new biller additions, bills due for payment and payments.
  • Biller Management
    Make payments to a variety of billers like mobile, insurance, landline, electricity etc.
